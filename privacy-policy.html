Privacy Policy

Carolina Property Management, LLC
Last updated: June 30, 2026

Carolina Property Management, LLC ("CPM," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and protect it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. It applies to applicants, residents, and prospective residents who interact with us, including anyone who provides a mobile phone number to receive text messages from us.

Scope

This policy covers personal information we collect in connection with our property management services, including through rental applications, lease agreements, our online tenant/applicant portal, email, phone, and text messaging.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide when you apply for, lease, or occupy a property we manage, and as needed to administer your tenancy, including:

  • Identity and contact details: name, mailing address, email address, and mobile and other phone numbers.
  • Application and lease information: information required to process your rental application, lease, and occupancy.
  • Account and communication records: records of messages, reminders, and notices we send to you and your responses.

How we collect your mobile number

We collect your mobile phone number when you provide it during the rental application and leasing process, including through our online applicant/tenant portal. By providing your mobile number, you agree that we may contact you about your tenancy as described in this policy and in our SMS Terms & Conditions.

How we use your information

  • Process rental applications and administer your lease and tenancy.
  • Communicate with you about your account, your residence, and your move-in, including reminders, account notices, and other operational messages.
  • Provide customer service and respond to your requests.
  • Maintain the security and integrity of our services.
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, accounting, and reporting obligations.

SMS / text messaging

If you provide a mobile phone number, you agree that we may send you account-related and operational text messages regarding your tenancy, such as move-in reminders and other service notifications.

  • Message frequency varies. These are occasional, transactional messages sent in connection with your tenancy.
  • Message and data rates may apply. Rates are charged by your mobile carrier.
  • Opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for help.
  • Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of leasing or of any purchase.

For full details, see our SMS Terms & Conditions.

We do not sell or share your mobile information

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or SMS opt-in information with third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile information will only be shared with service providers that help us deliver the messaging service, solely to operate the service, and not for any other purpose. No mobile information is shared with third parties for marketing. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes under any circumstances.

Service providers we use

We share personal information only with trusted service providers who process it on our behalf to operate our services, and only to the extent necessary, including:

  • Property management software (AppFolio): to manage applications, leases, resident records, and contact information.
  • Messaging provider (Twilio): to deliver text messages, such as move-in reminders, to the mobile number you provide.

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and to use it only to provide services to us. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Data retention and security

We retain personal information for as long as needed to administer your tenancy and to meet legal, accounting, or reporting requirements, after which we dispose of it securely. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Your choices and rights

  • You may update your contact information or ask questions about this policy by contacting us using the details below.
  • You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP.
  • Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

Children's privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be posted on this page.
